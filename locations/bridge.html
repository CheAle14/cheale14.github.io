Tower Bridge

Location

Tower Bridge spans the 244m gap across the River Thames beside the Tower of London, joining the boroughs of Tower Hamlets and Southwark

Trivia

The bridge, opened 30th June 1984, is a bascule and suspension bridge. This means the road portion may be raised by use of a counterweight to allow taller boats to pass underneath
The bridge is often called "London Bridge", however London Bridge refers to various bridges that have spanned the Thames between the City of London and Southwark.
