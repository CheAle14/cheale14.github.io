Palace of Westminster

Location

The Palace of Westminster is a grade I listed building located within London, the capital of the United Kingdom.
The Palace directly overlooks the River Thames, and is adjacent to Westminster bridge.

Purpose

The Palace is the seat of the United Kingdom's legislature, within the palace are the chambers of the House of Commons and the House of Lords, the two chambers that comprise the United Kingdom's Parliament, the sovereign and supreme body that creates, amends and repeals UK laws.
